About Me

My name is Eric Anderson. I am a front-end and back-end web developer living in the Philippines, based out of San Diego, California USA. I specialize in Django Framework, UX/UI, and Responsive Web Design.

Before I becoming a developer, I was a chef for over twenty years. While being a chef, woking in hot kitchens taught me two import lessons: to excel under pressure, and to consistently strive for excellence.

A year ago, utilizing the above lessons, I decided to realize my teenage dream of being a web developer. I became proficient in front-end CSS and Javascript, and back-end Python and Django. Using these technologies, I strive to create professional, beautiful, user-friendly, and responsive websites.
